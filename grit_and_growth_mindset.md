# Grit and Growth Mindset

## 1. Grit

**Question 1 - Explain about grit and its importance?**

**Answer:**  
Grit is the combination of passion and perseverance for long-term goals, showing that sustained effort matters more than talent alone in achieving success.

---

## 2. Introduction to Growth Mindset

**Question 2 - Explain about growth mindset?**

**Answer:**  
A growth mindset is the belief that abilities and intelligence can be developed through effort, learning, and persistence, rather than being fixed traits.

---

## 3. Understanding Internal Locus of Control


**Question 3 - What is the Internal Locus of Control?**

**Answer:**  
Internal Locus of Control is the belief that you can influence your own outcomes through your actions and decisions. The key point of the video is that taking responsibility for your success and failures helps maintain motivation and control over your life.

---

## 4. How to Build a Growth Mindset

**Question 4 - What are the key points mentioned by the speaker to build a growth mindset?**

**Answer:**  
- Embrace challenges  
- Persist through obstacles  
- Learn from criticism  
- Celebrate effort, not just results  
- Focus on learning rather than proving yourself  
- Replace "failing" with "learning."  

---

## 5. Mindset - A MountBlue Warrior Reference Manual

**Question 5 - What are your ideas to take action and build a Growth Mindset?**

**Answer:**  
- Set learning-focused goals rather than outcome-focused goals  
- Seek feedback actively and use it for improvement  
- Reflect on failures and extract lessons  
- Practice consistently and embrace difficult tasks  
- Surround yourself with mentors and peers who encourage growth  
- Track personal progress to stay motivated and celebrate small wins  
- Replace negative self-talk with positive, effort-oriented thinking
