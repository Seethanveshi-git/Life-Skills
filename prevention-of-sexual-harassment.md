# Prevention of Sexual Harassment


### Question 1 - What kinds of behaviour cause sexual harassment?

**Answer:**  
- **Verbal / Communication:**  
  - Sexually suggestive remarks, jokes, or comments about appearance or private life  
  - Unwelcome sexual advances or invitations  
  - Inappropriate questions or comments about someone’s sex life  

- **Non-verbal / Visual:**  
  - Displaying sexual images, messages, or emails  
  - Sending offensive pictures or digital content  

- **Physical Conduct:**  
  - Unwanted touch such as hugging, pinching, brushing, or caressing  
  - Kissing or other inappropriate physical contact  

---

### Question 2 - What would you do in case you face or witness any incident or repeated incidents of such behaviour?

**Answer:**  
- **Immediate Response (If Safe):**  
  - Clearly tell the person the behaviour is unwelcome  
  - Document what happened: date, time, place, description, witnesses  

- **Report Internally:**  
  - Refer to organisation’s POSH policy  
  - Report to Human Resources or Internal Complaints Committee (ICC)  
  - File a formal written complaint if required  

- **Follow-up Actions:**  
  - Cooperate with any inquiry or investigation  
  - Request support measures (interim transfer, leave, adjustments)  
  - Escalate to external authorities if the organisation fails to act  

- **If You Witness It:**  
  - Support the affected person and encourage them to report  
  - Report yourself if appropriate or if the person is unwilling to file
